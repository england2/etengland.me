# Introduction

While this site serves as a portfolio for some projects, I also want it to be detailed enough to where other people trying to learn software developement can benefit from it.

Even though it's a very simple and common usecase, there /isn't really a full article that explains how to set up even a dead-simple microservice CI/CD system onto a Kubernetes cluster./ There's also a few knowledge gaps regarding the differentation between the developement and build process of microservice software/ and the continuous integration and deployment aspects of it.


It's my hope that this article can fill these knowledge gaps and serve as a decent reference for someone trying to build a dead-simple microservice CI/CD system
